Captain America

What's more American than Nascar, Alabama Football, and spicy barbecue wings you ask?
Let me answer that in two words for you, Captain America.
With both sweet and nimble moves Captain America is a clear Marvel favorite.
A man who puts himself before others? A true man of service? A guy like this isn't even a once in a lifetime catch (because he isn't real).
In all seriousness I don't know much about any comics (I know nothing), but Captain America looks pretty cool.
